Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about scheduling, billing, and our services.

1. How do I book a ride?
You can schedule a ride online via the "Book a Ride" page, or call us at 276-623-7970 during business hours. We'll confirm pickup time and any special needs.
2. Do you provide wheelchair-accessible vehicles?
Yes — we operate lift-equipped vehicles and trained staff to assist wheelchair users. Please specify mobility needs when booking so we can assign the right vehicle.
3. What areas do you serve?
We provide non-emergency medical transportation across our listed service areas. For long-distance requests, contact dispatch to confirm availability and pricing.
4. How is payment handled?
We accept cash, card, and some insurance/Medicaid programs where applicable. Payment and billing options will be confirmed when booking - contact billing for special arrangements.
5. What if my appointment time changes?
Please notify us as soon as possible. For time changes within 24 hours of pickup, call our dispatch team so we can reschedule and minimize wait times.
6. Are your drivers trained for medical assistance?
Our drivers are trained in safe patient handling and basic assistance. For clients requiring medical attendants, please inform us so we can accommodate clinical needs.
7. How do I file feedback or a complaint?
We welcome feedback. Email inquiries@activecaretransit.com or call 276-623-7970 and ask to speak with customer service. We'll respond promptly and work to resolve any concerns.
